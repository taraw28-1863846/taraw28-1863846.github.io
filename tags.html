About Me

I was born in Hannover, Germany and, since my mom is German and my dad is American,
I have dual citizenship. I lived in Germany for about 2 years after I was born, and
then my parents moved my sister and I to New Zealand for another 2 years. After that, we
went back to Germany for a year before moving to Venice, Florida for 12 years. My parents
have always loved traveling and I have definitely inherited that trait. I have explored
various regions of the USA; Colorado, Wyoming, and Montana are some of my favorites.

I am currently a Sophomore at UW. I am an intended CSE major however I am seriously
considering becoming a Linguistics major as I love languages. I am currently somewhat
fluent in German and intend to start learning ASL next school year. This quarter, I am taking
INFO 101 (Social Networking), CSE 154 (Web Programming) and PHIL 120 (Introduction to Logic).
